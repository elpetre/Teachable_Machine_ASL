Welcome to ASL Numbers. Using a new tool called Teachable Machine, I have created an American Sign Language Teacher fully curated from artificial intelligence.

This machine will be able to identify ASL number 3. To many children growing up, the symbols for 3 for counting vs. 3 for ASL. I created this in order to settle the difference.

If you sign for 3 with your pointer, middle, and ring finger, it will tell you to try again, until you sign 3 in ASL

This is a prototype so please be patient. She's doing her best. :)
